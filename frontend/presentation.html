Project Presentation

The
Archive

A sophisticated digital library system built with modern technologies.

01 / Introduction

What is
The Archive

The Archive is an elegant, full-stack application that lets you discover, store, and organize books effortlessly.

Think of it as your personal library in the digital age, combining the beauty of classic design with modern technology and speed.

02 / Architecture

How It Works

01

Client Interface

Beautiful frontend where you manage your collection with intuitive controls and highly legible editorial typography.

02

API Backend

RESTful server handling all operations—creating, reading, and updating your records seamlessly over HTTP.

03

Database

A robust SQLite storage engine keeping your book records safe, organized, and instantly accessible.

04

Real-time Sync

Every change you make in the client instantly reflects in the database, with a verified live connection status.

03 / Capabilities

Key Features.

Designed for simplicity, built for power. Managing your digital library has never been this fluid.

I. Add Books Quickly add new titles with book name and author info.
II. Browse Collection View all your books in an organized, searchable list.
III. Delete Records Remove books when they no longer belong in your archive.
IV. Live Database Every action persists instantly to your SQLite database.
